About Island Boy Kreationz Virgin Islands born and raised

Hi, I'm Deon Henry.

Island Boy Kreationz started with Virgin Islands roots, Charlotte hustle, prayer, and a small $500 leap into food.

The journey was not smooth. Deon lost $12,000 in his first trailer scam, then kept building. Over the years, his trucks and trailers were stolen five times, but the vision never stopped.

That is why every plate carries more than flavor. It carries faith, consistency, and a story people remember.

From a tent, two tables, and a cooler to 100,000+ plates served.

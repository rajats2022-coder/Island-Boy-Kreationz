## Current competitive result patterns

- [Charlotte Food Truck Association](https://cltfta.org/what-we-do) emphasizes event coordination and a broad truck network.
- [Best Food Trucks Charlotte Caribbean catering](https://www.bestfoodtrucks.com/food-trucks/catering/charlotte/cuisine/caribbean) competes on marketplace convenience and category discovery.
- [J&J Jamaican Food Truck](https://jamaicanfoodtruckcharlotte.com/) competes directly on Jamaican cuisine plus Charlotte food-truck intent.
- [Ms. Didi's Caribbean Kitchen](https://www.msdidiskitchen.com/cateringandbooking) presents clear catering and booking pathways.
- [Chateau Creole](https://www.chateauxcreolerestaurant.com/) competes on Caribbean/Creole menu discovery and restaurant authority.

Island Boy's defensible differentiation is its real founder story, Virgin Islands roots, recognizable oxtail/wings/bowls menu, recurring Hunter Avenue and Amazon schedule, truck-or-trays flexibility, no-pork kitchen, and strong owner media library. The site and GBP should convert that proof rather than imitate competitor claims.

## Guideline decisions applied

- The GBP business name stays the real-world name shown on the logo and website; no keyword stuffing.
- The mobile-truck schedule remains explicit and change-aware. Saturday is not represented as a fixed daytime location.
- Only the four live GBP service areas receive location landing pages and area schema.
- Menu prices come only from the current website order data. Catering pricing remains quote-based.
- Instagram schedule posts are reused only when the real caption qualifies as a fresh schedule announcement, and the shortcode is deduplicated.
- Review requests use honest-review language and never promise incentives or request a particular star rating.

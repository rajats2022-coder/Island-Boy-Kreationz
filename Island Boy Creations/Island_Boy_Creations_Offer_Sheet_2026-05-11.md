## Lead Growth + Google Visibility

A website matters because it gives the business a real online home that is open 24/7. Social media is important, but a website gives customers one clear place to see the food, story, menu/services, contact form, booking options, reviews, photos, and next steps.

For local businesses, the website can help turn attention into leads. When someone searches for food, catering, events, or the business name, the goal is to make it easy for them to call, message, fill out a form, or book. We have had clients make their website investment back in less than 24 hours by getting leads from the site and online presence. Results depend on the offer, market, traffic, and follow-up, so this is not a guarantee, but it shows what can happen when the website is built to convert.

The monthly partnership can also include Google Business Profile management. That means helping the business show up better on Google Maps and local search with stronger photos, service details, updates, descriptions, keywords, review strategy, and accurate business information.

**SEO** means Search Engine Optimization. It helps the business show up when people search on Google for things like Caribbean food, catering, food truck, local eats, or the business name.

**AEO** means Answer Engine Optimization. It prepares the website and business information so AI tools, voice search, Google answers, and future search assistants can understand what the business does and recommend it clearly.

In today’s world, people do not only search one way. They use Google, Maps, Instagram, TikTok, AI search, voice assistants, and reviews. The goal is to make Island Boy easy to find, easy to trust, and easy to contact everywhere customers are looking.

How it works.

When trays beat the truck

Indoor venues, office break rooms, church halls, tight parking, short windows, or any event where you just want the food handled.

How trays arrive

Cooked, covered, and ready to serve. Tell the team your serving time and they aim the drop-off so food lands hot.

What to send

Date, drop-off address, guest count, serving time, and menu direction — mains, sides, and desserts.

What lands on the table.

Oxtail trays, wing trays, chopped chicken, curry chicken, rice and peas, yellow rice, cabbage, candied yams, mac and cheese, cornbread, cakes, and desserts.

Booking, step by step.

Send the details

Date, address, guest count, service window, and menu direction — through the form, or by call or text at 980-785-8372.

The team reviews fit

Event fit, travel, timing, and menu get checked before anything is quoted — no surprises on either side.

Lock the date

Once the window is confirmed, the truck rolls or the trays land — hot, on time, and portioned for your crowd.

Good to know: Trays are the most flexible way to book. Confirmed service areas are Charlotte, Concord, Gastonia, and Huntersville; other addresses require direct confirmation.

Quick answers.

How many people does a tray feed?

Depends on the dish and whether it is a half or full tray — send your headcount and the team sizes the order.

Do trays come with serving gear?

Ask in your inquiry — setups vary by event, and the team will tell you exactly what arrives.

Can I pick up instead?

Pickup can work depending on the day — ask when you send your date.
